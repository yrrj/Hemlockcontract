Expert Equipment Maintenance & Technical Support

Hemlock Contracting Ltd., based in Vancouver, BC, is a leading North American contracting and service company specializing in expert equipment maintenance, technical support, and project implementation. With over 10 engineers across Canada and the US, Hemlock serves a diverse clientele, including mining labs and government agencies.
