Expert Equipment Maintenance & Technical Support

Hemlock Contracting Ltd, headquartered in Vancouver, British Columbia, is a leading contracting and service company in North America, specializing in professional equipment maintenance, technical support and project implementation. Hemlock has an experienced team of engineers in Canada and the United States, providing services to a wide range of clients, including mining laboratories and government agencies. Our main services include advanced photon analysis technology support based on a linear accelerator (LINAC), Ion Mobility Spectrometry (IMS), and related installation services. We are committed to providing high-level operation and proactive services to ensure the success of every project.
